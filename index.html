Common questions

Answered.

How fast can you actually deliver a lead list? +
Standard is 24 hours from the moment you send a brief. If you need it same-day, ask — we can often accommodate. If for any reason a list takes longer, you'll hear from us before the deadline, not after.
What industries and niches do you cover? +
Any niche with an online presence — real estate agents, SaaS companies, dental practices, law firms, e-commerce stores, recruitment agencies, restaurants, gyms. If the data exists publicly online, we can scrape and structure it. We've never turned down a niche for being too specific.
Can I see a sample before I pay? +
Yes. Email us with your target niche and location and we'll send back 10–20 verified sample records at no cost. You'll know exactly what format, fields, and quality to expect before committing to a full list.
Where are you based and do you work internationally? +
We're based in Pakistan and work remotely with clients across the US, UK, Europe, and beyond. Our timezone (PKT, UTC+5) means a brief sent at end-of-business in the US often arrives done by the time you're back at your desk next morning.
What if a deliverable doesn't match what was agreed? +
We fix it or refund it. Every project is scoped in writing before work begins — deliverables, timeline, format. If the output doesn't match the written scope, that's on us and we make it right. No arguments.
Do you do ongoing work or one-off projects only? +
Both. Lead lists and small scraping jobs work well as one-off orders. Scraping pipelines and software development tend to be ongoing — pipelines run monthly, and software projects often have follow-on work. We're happy to work either way. No contracts unless a project scope requires it.
